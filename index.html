Trimbakeshwar Temple

📍 Nashik, Maharashtra
Hindu Maharashtra AR Available
About this place
One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, Trimbakeshwar is a sacred Shiva temple near Nashik where the holy Godavari river originates. The temple holds immense religious significance in Hindu tradition and attracts millions of pilgrims each year.

Did You Know?
The Trimbakeshwar Shiva Lingam has three faces representing Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva. It is the only Jyotirlinga with this unique characteristic.
